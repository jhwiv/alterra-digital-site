The Gap

The planning was excellent.
The delivery didn't match.

A couple spends $35,000 on a two-week trip. The advisor spends 40 hours planning every detail. Then they deliver all of that work as a PDF attached to an email. The client prints it, loses it, can't find the restaurant phone number at 8 PM in Zürich, mispronounces the reservation name, doesn't know it's about to rain, and calls the advisor three times during dinner.

The PDF Experience
  • Static — no live weather or real-time updates
  • Printed and lost, or buried in an email thread
  • No way to find the nearest pharmacy at midnight
  • Clients call the advisor with questions that are already answered
The Digital Companion
  • Live weather with hourly narratives written in plain English
  • One link. Adds to home screen like a native app — no download
  • Location-aware search finds what clients need from wherever they stand
  • Fewer "what now?" calls. More referrals. Higher perceived value.

Inside Every Companion

Twelve features your clients will actually use

Every companion is built by hand, not generated by a template. Each feature below appears in both live demos — audited, tested, and polished before your client opens the link.

Day‑by‑Day Itinerary

Time-stamped cards for every stop, reservation, and transit connection. Confirmed reservations clearly marked with guest counts and booking details. Contextual notes and insider tips throughout.

Live Weather Forecasts

Hourly narrative forecasts written in plain English: "Bring layers for the mountain morning. Rain chance after 3 PM." Expands to a full phone-frame weather modal with contextual packing advice.

360° Street Views

Fully interactive, navigable Google Street View for every hotel, restaurant, and landmark. Your clients virtually walk the streets, find the entrance, and see the neighborhood before they arrive.

Pronunciation Guide

One tap on any foreign place name or phrase plays perfect audio using the device's built-in speech engine. Works offline. Your clients walk into a Zürich café and confidently say "Grüezi" instead of fumbling.

Location‑Aware Search

One tap finds the nearest coffee shop, pharmacy, ATM, or restaurant from wherever your client is standing. Quick-tap presets for ten common travel needs. No app-switching, no copying addresses.

AI Trip Concierge

A built-in AI chat tied directly to the itinerary. "What's for dinner tonight?" "Nearest hospital?" "Today's drive?" Quick-action prompts surface the most common questions instantly, no typing required.

Offline‑Ready PWA

Clients add it to their home screen with a single tap — it opens full-screen, no browser chrome. The full itinerary, reservations, directions, and pronunciation guide work without cell service. Live content refreshes online.

Flight & Departure Alerts

Timely reminders that surface exactly when needed — not clutter until then. "Leave hotel by 6:00 PM · Uber 15 min to ZRH" pinned at the top of the relevant day, in red when time is critical.

Interactive Route Map

Full-screen Leaflet map with every stop plotted and color-coded by day. Overnight stops in navy, waypoints in gold. Driving routes as solid lines, ferry crossings as dashed. Filter by day to zoom to each destination.

Multi-Timezone Clock

A persistent floating clock showing home time and local time simultaneously — essential for multi-country road trips and international flights. Keeps clients and advisors aligned without mental arithmetic.

Emergency & Essentials

Emergency numbers formatted for the destination country, embassy contacts, nearest hospital, disruption playbook (rail strikes, weather alternates), and a curated survival guide — always one tap away, even offline.

White-Label Branding

Your agency logo, color palette, fonts, and style guide applied to every companion. Clients see your brand, not ours. Delivered as a unique URL — shareable, bookmarkable, and always up to date.
